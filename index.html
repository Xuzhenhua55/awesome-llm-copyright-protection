LLM Copyright Protection Research

Welcome to the LLM Copyright Protection Research project. This repository contains research and resources related to protecting intellectual property in large language models.

Research Areas

  • Model Fingerprinting
  • Fingerprint Transfer

Latest Updates

This project aims to explore and develop methods for protecting intellectual property in large language models through various techniques including:

  • Model fingerprinting and watermarking
  • Transfer learning for copyright protection
  • Detection and removal of unauthorized model usage
