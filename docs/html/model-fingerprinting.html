Model Fingerprinting

Model fingerprinting is a technique used to identify and track large language models through unique characteristics in their outputs.

Non-intrusive Methods

Non-intrusive fingerprinting methods do not require modifications to the model's architecture or training process.

Intrusive Methods

Intrusive fingerprinting methods involve modifications to the model's architecture or training process.
